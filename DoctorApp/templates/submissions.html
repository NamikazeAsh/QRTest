About Us

What is SSO (Surgery Second Opinion)?

A surgery second opinion is a medical consultation sought by a patient who has already received a diagnosis or treatment plan from one physician and wants another expert's evaluation. Here's how the process generally works:

  1. Patient receives initial diagnosis and treatment recommendation.
  2. Patient seeks second opinion for confirmation or alternative options.
  3. Patient gathers medical records.
  4. Patient finds another qualified surgeon or specialist.
  5. Patient schedules appointment with second opinion provider.
  6. Medical records are shared with the second opinion provider and second opinion provider reviews the records.
  7. Patient meets with second opinion provider for consultation.
  8. Provider may offer alternative diagnosis or treatment approach.
  9. Patient receives second opinion and asks questions.
  10. Patient makes an informed decision based on both opinions.
